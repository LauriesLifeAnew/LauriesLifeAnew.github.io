I promise, you have a new life ahead of you.

I have had bi-polar depression for 40 years, through a my college years, fantastic marriage, and satisfying work. I recently had relief from TMS. But along the way, I found some things to do that helped me keep trying, keep me busy. Here they are:

Around the house

  • Tidy something
    • hang up clothes
    • stack books
    • organize a small area

Outside

  • Listen to something
    • Meditative music
    • Talking: TED, podcasts, books. No news.
  • Move
    • set your timer for 5 min
    • walk different paces

Anywhere

  • Listen to something
    • Meditative music
    • Talking: TED, podcasts, books. No news.
